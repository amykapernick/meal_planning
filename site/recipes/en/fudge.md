---
title: Fudge
categories:
  - Christmas
  - Dessert
ingredients:
  - 525g chocolate
  - 1 can condensed milk
  - 57.5g butter
  - 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
---
1. Heat chocolate, condensed milk and butter over boiling water. Stir until smooth
2. Stir in nuts, pour into greased baking dish
3. Refrigerate until set
